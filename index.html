Who We Are

Care Club Welfare has served the communities of Asansol and many other parts in West Bengal India for more than ten years. A fully certified NGO, CCWT has focused on helping those least capable of helping themselves; programs assist families, but HAVE a particular emphasis on investing in children.

Projects combine a high degree of local self-enablement, local initiative (all are conceived/initiated locally), holistic service models and an extraordinary commitment to accountability and transparency.

Our Mission

Our mission and vision are to implement a growing social change that is focused on educating and empowering underprivileged children through advanced methodologies. We also want to make people take responsibility for the depriving education quality of these children. We want to ensure that children have a happy and bright present with well-set future goals with their rights being protected and honored in the society making them equally eligible for every opportunity.
